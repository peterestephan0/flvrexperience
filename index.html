Our Story

Two crafts. One table.

FLVR was born from two lifelong passions — one for exceptional food, one for unforgettable experiences. Since 2017, Peter has trained with chefs across Egypt and internationally to perfect FLVR's signature dough. Yasmine, a professional DJ, brings the instinct for atmosphere, timing, and reading a room. Together, they don't simply serve food — they help create experiences people remember.

Peter Estephan
Co-Founder & Operations Director

Since 2017, Peter has immersed himself in sourdough and Neapolitan pizza, attending workshops and masterclasses with respected local and international pizza chefs. His experimentation led to FLVR's signature dough — a refined recipe with proprietary ingredients and technique. Alongside this culinary journey, Peter built a successful international career leading customer experience, digital transformation, and organizational change initiatives. Designing seamless experiences, understanding people's needs, and creating memorable interactions became part of his professional DNA.

Yasmine Wahby
Co-Founder & Experience Director

Yasmine is a professional DJ whose career has been built around creating atmosphere and unforgettable moments. She understands something equally important — the energy of an event. Reading the room. Understanding timing. Creating atmosphere. Knowing exactly when and how to engage people. Her understanding of audience energy, entertainment, and event dynamics shapes every FLVR experience — from music and flow to guest interaction — ensuring every event feels alive and aligned with the client's vision.

"One from behind the oven. One from behind the decks." Peter Estephan & Yasmine Wahby, Co-Founders

Our Mobile Setup

Built to fit anywhere.

FLVR travels on a custom-built portable booth, roughly 2 meters in length — matte black, warm, and architectural.

  • Runs as a single piece for most venues
  • Splits into two smaller sections for tight spaces — a balcony, a small garden, an indoor corner, a boat deck
  • Keeps its clean, controlled look either way — no compromise on how the pizza is made or presented
  • Says yes to almost any venue, without asking you to redesign your space around us
